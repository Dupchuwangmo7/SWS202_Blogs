### **2. What is a Regular Expression?**

A regular expression is a sequence of characters that defines a **search pattern**. These patterns use **metacharacters**â€”special symbols that provide rules for matching characters.

They are supported in many programming languages and tools such as `grep`, `sed`, `awk`, and more.
